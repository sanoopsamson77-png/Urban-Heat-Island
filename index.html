üå°Ô∏è Land Surface Temperature (LST) Calculation

LST is derived from Landsat 8 Thermal Infrared Sensor (TIRS) Band 10 using the following methodology:

  • Radiometric Calibration: DN values converted to Top-of-Atmosphere radiance
  • Atmospheric Correction: Level 2 products include atmospheric correction
  • Temperature Conversion: Brightness temperature calculated using Planck's equation
  • Scale Factor: ST_B10 √ó 0.00341802 + 149.0 (Kelvin), then converted to Celsius

üåø Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI)

NDVI quantifies vegetation health and density:

Formula: NDVI = (NIR - Red) / (NIR + Red)

  • NIR: Landsat 8 Band 5 (Near-Infrared)
  • Red: Landsat 8 Band 4 (Visible Red)
  • Range: -1 to +1 (higher values indicate denser vegetation)

üèôÔ∏è Urban Heat Island (UHI) Detection

UHI hotspots are identified using statistical analysis:

  • Calculate mean and standard deviation of LST across the analysis area
  • Identify pixels exceeding 1.5 standard deviations above the mean
  • These statistically significant warm anomalies indicate UHI zones
  • Results correlate with built-up density and vegetation deficit
